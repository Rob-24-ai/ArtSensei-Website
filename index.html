The feedback problem

You make work. You want honest, thoughtful responses—not "nice colors!" or generic tips from YouTube. But substantive critique is hard to find. Art school ended. Your community scattered. Open critiques feel awkward. And most people, even well-meaning ones, don't know how to look at a painting.

You need someone who can actually see what's happening in your work.

A knowledgeable studio visitor

ArtSensei is an AI trained to give the kind of critique you'd get from a thoughtful peer or mentor—someone who knows formal analysis, understands art history, and can articulate what's working in your piece and what isn't. Built by artists who studied and taught at SAIC, Yale, NYAA, RISD, and MICA.

What makes it different

Structured, not superficial

Feedback organized around formal principles—the same framework serious artists use to evaluate work.

Conversational

Not a one-way assessment. Ask questions, disagree, request different angles. The critique evolves with you.

Art-historically grounded

References real works and real principles. Connects your decisions to the broader tradition.

Available anytime

No scheduling. No social dynamics. Just thoughtful feedback when you need it—2pm or 2am.

Remembers your practice

Tracks your work and ongoing concerns across sessions. Understands your trajectory, not just isolated pieces.

Built for people who make work

ArtSensei is for professional painters, art students, and committed hobbyists—people who show up to the studio and want to get better. If you're looking for tutorials on how to draw an eye, this isn't for you. If you wish you had access to sharper critique but don't have a program or community that provides it, we built this for you.
